Tourist Places to go in Surabaya

Surabaya may be one of the cities in Indonesia that is closely related to history. No wonder, there are many tourist attractions in Surabaya that are related to the history of the struggle of the heroes of independence in the city nicknamed the City of Heroes. But not only that, Surabaya has a myriad of tourist attractions that offer education and of course entertainment. Let's take a look at a series of recommendations for tourist attractions in Surabaya that are hits and worth visiting.

Tugu Pahlawan

This monument is a very popular icon of Surabaya. Tugu Pahlawan is surrounded by shady greenery, perfect for a relaxing spot after touring the city of Surabaya. This towering monument has been standing since the colonial period.

Charge : free

Surabaya Zoo

A tourist attraction in Surabaya that is always crowded and in demand is the zoo. Located in the city center, Surabaya Zoo is always crowded every day. This zoo is one of the oldest in Indonesia, you know.

Charge : Rp. 15.000,00
