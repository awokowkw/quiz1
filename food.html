Rujak Cingur

It consists of a vegetable stew of kale and bean sprouts, in addition to fresh cucumber, bengkuang, and young mango. It also comes with slices of soybean tempeh, tofu, rice cake, and of course cingur.

Tahu Campur

It consists of chewy beef soup, beef tendon, fried tofu, cassava cake, fresh bean sprouts, fresh watercress, yellow noodles, and shrimp crackers.
